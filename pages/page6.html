Choose the optimal implant to fit your patient's individual needs.

Who is a potentially appropriate augmentation patient for the Natrelle® 410?

May be an appropriate patient

  • Desires a proportionate augmentation with less upper pole fullness
  • Is postpartum, with minimal ptosis
  • Has minimal breast coverage
  • Has tighter skin envelope
  • Has tuberous or constricted breast

May not be an appropriate patient

  • Desires an overly exaggerated upper pole
  • Has very loose skin envelope
  • Wants to be very large breasted
  • Has pectus excavatum
  • Has pectus carinatum
  • Has significant ptosis, where a mastopexy
    is indicated
  • Has severe asymmetries
  • Is a revision patient (until surgeon has more experience with the Natrelle® 410)
